About Us

Have you ever wanted to go hiking but didn't know where? Or have you ever been under-prepared for a hike?


Well then Hikipedia is the solution for you! Our web application will combine trail, weather, and map APIs to give you detailed descriptions and data about the trail, the weather you might encounter, what clothes and materials to bring, how many calories you will burn, how much water you should drink, and much more to help you plan the perfect trek.

Hikipedia is the one stop information hub for everything hiking and exploring related! Utilize our service to plan your next hike with friends and family, all while exploring local culture, food, and activities.

Frequenty Asked Questions

How do I search for a hike around me?

You can find hiking trails by clicking on the "Search" page on the navigation bar at the top of this screen or by going to the "Home" page and clicking on "Find Your Hike." In both cases, you will have to type in your desired trail location and hit search to find hikes around it.

How do I apply filters to my search?

Once your have successfully searched for a trail location, you can use the "Filters" button underneath the search bar to filter the trail results by intensity, trail type, distance to the input location, etc.

How can I see a visual map of a specific trail and find directions to it?

To see a map of a specific hiking trail in your search results, simply click on it. That will take you to a trail information page that includes its map and an option to "Get Directions."

Can I check the weather for a specific trail?

Yes! To do this, click on any trail in your search results and you will automatically be taken to a trail information page that includes a map and thorough description of it. Once you are in the trail information page, click on the "Plan for Trek" button, which will take you to a new page where you can see the weather for the next few days at the chosen trail.

How does the website determine the recommended calorie intake for each trail?

Our website uses an algorithm that takes into account a trail's intensity combined with the user's weight to calculate the number of calories said user is expected to burn, and then uses this value to create personalized recommnedations for every user and trail.
